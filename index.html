About Us

EcoDen is an E-waste management product facilitating in urban and remote parts of Kerala.

We do the best management of waste here.

E-waste management is defined as a method to remove e-waste from the environment in a safe and scientific manner to avoid its negative damaging effects on the environment. It includes recycling, refurbishing, and reusing e-waste that has outlived its usefulness. Our company succeeds in collecting E-wastes from houses to houses as their needs and recycle them into useful household products. We forsee to collect 20 tonness of E-wates by 2025. The products that are made through E-wastes are of great quality.

When e-Waste is not treated properly, it can be very dangerous and contain compounds and materials such as toxic substances and organic pollutants that have a severe impact on human health and ecosystems. Electronic items, for example, are frequently burned at informal garbage sites, releasing toxins into the air that harm both humans and ecology. I t is not only the environment or the people who live in proximity to the trash sites who are at risk. The problem of e-Waste stems primarily from developed countries that perceive recycling e-Waste to be too pricey in their own countries and hence happily ship it to impoverished countries to be handled, with very no background check on how the e-Waste would be treated.

Through EcoDen we are succeeding in collecting E-wastes in a more effective manner.
